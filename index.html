Zombie Survival

Enter Game

Zombie Defense Survival is a zombie survival game. Where you defend a structure called "Main Hut" from zombie-like enemies. You defend the structure by building or attacking the enemies and you only lose when the structure is destroyed.

How to Play

WASD - Movement
Space - Spam Attack/Build
Number Keys - Hold Building
Click - Attack/Build
C - Mark Current Location
Q - Heal
E - Auto Attack

Tips and Info:

1. Explore the map!
2. Play with friends!
3. Beware of monsters near "farming" locations.
4. Enemy Turret Projectiles can still damage you even if you have emp effect.
5. AG is hard to obtain, so be careful to spend them on good items.
6. Most loot is obtained by "drops", collet them before they despawn!
7. Zombies with emp effect can be tricky to kill, use spikes or buy a turret that can target emp zombies.
